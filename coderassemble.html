Coders Assemble

Where Quick Thinking meets Swift Coding!


Coders Assemble is a team-based coding competition where each team consists of two members: a Coder and a Bidder. The Bidder estimates the time required to solve a given problem, and the Coder must solve the problem within the specified time.

Rules and Regulations

Round 1

  1. Each team must consist of two members: one coder and one bidder.
  2. A coding problem will be displayed to the bidder. The bidder will have a limited time to read and analyze the problem.
  3. The bidder will propose a time constraint within which they believe the coder can solve the problem.
  4. Once the time is locked, it cannot be changed.
  5. The coder will be presented with the problem after the bidding phase.
  6. The coder must solve the problem within the time constraint set by the bidder.
  7. The coder will not know the time constraint beforehand.
  8. If the coder fails to submit within the time, the team will score fewer points for that round.
  9. Participants must use the coding environment provided by the organizers.
  10. Use of external resources or collaboration with other teams is prohibited.
