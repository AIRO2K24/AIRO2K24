Prompt With Wakanda

"Unleash Your Creativity, Strengthen Your Team!"


Prompt with Wakanda is an engaging team-based game that emphasizes creativity and communication. Participants collaborate to decipher visual clues and use AI technology for real-time verification. The game fosters a dynamic environment where creative thinking and effective communication are key. Teams work together to interpret images and scenarios, promoting teamwork and innovative problem-solving. Whether strategizing with colleagues or competing against other teams, Prompt with Wakanda offers a fun and stimulating experience that enhances participantsâ€™ ability to work cohesively and think creatively.

Rules and Regulations

  1. Each team must consist of three members: a Clue Finder, an Illustrator, and a Prompt Enterer.
  2. The Clue Finder receives a set of images and must determine the connection between images.
  3. The Clue Finder communicates the answer to the Illustrator verbally.
  4. The Illustrator then draws an image representing the answer.
  5. The Prompt Enterer observes the drawing and formulates a descriptive and indirect prompt to the AI Language Model.
  6. Teams earn points if the AI Language Model provides the correct answer based on the prompt.
  7. Each team will need to solve 5 sets of images within the allotted time.
  8. No text is allowed in the drawing.
  9. No direct hints or written clues are allowed between the Clue Finder and the Prompt Enterer.
  10. All participants must adhere to the rules and maintain fairness throughout the game.
  11. Any form of cheating or rule-breaking will result in disqualification.
