About the Department of AI-DS

The Department of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science at Sri Sairam Engineering College is a vibrant hub of innovation and learning, dedicated to advancing the frontiers of AI and data science. Our curriculum is meticulously designed to equip overalls with a robust foundation in AI principles, data analysis, machine learning, and related technologies. We emphasize hands-on learning, encouraging overalls to engage in real-world projects and research initiatives that address contemporary challenges. Our faculty comprises experienced professionals and academics who bring a wealth of knowledge and industry insights to the classroom. With state-of-the-art laboratories, a strong focus on interdisciplinary collaboration, and a commitment to fostering creativity and critical thinking, our department aims to produce skilled graduates who are prepared to lead in the ever-evolving landscape of AI and data science.
