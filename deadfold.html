Dead Fold

"Together in Laughter: Building Bonds, Creating Joy!"


This event is designed to bring joy and excitement to all participants. It features a variety of engaging activities and games that encourage laughter, camaraderie, and a spirited atmosphere. The event emphasizes teamwork, communication, and trust as the participants work together to achieve their goal.

Rules & Regulations

  1. Each team must contain only 2 members.
  2. No external help or communication devices are allowed during the event.
  3. Any team found receiving outside assistance will be disqualified.
  4. The event organisers reserve the right to modify the rules and format if necessary.
  5. Any form of inappropriate behaviour, including arguing, cheating, or disrupting the event, will result in immediate disqualification.
  6. Points will be awarded based on performance, teamwork, and adherence to the event's spirit. Teams with the highest scores will be declared the winners.
