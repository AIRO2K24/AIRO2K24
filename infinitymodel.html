Infinity Models

"Where Innovation Takes Shape and Imagination Knows No Limits!"


Ignite your imagination at Infinity Models! Join us for an electrifying technical model exhibition where groundbreaking ideas and innovation collide. Showcase your unique projects in technology, science, engineering, and beyond. Meet fellow enthusiasts, share tips, and learn from master model makers. Be part of this exciting event where imagination meets technology and the next big breakthrough begins!

Guidelines/Rules

  1. Each team shall consist of 2-3 members.
  2. Teams can work on any domain (hardware and/or software).
  3. Teams must bring all necessary equipment, including laptops and prototypes, to showcase their projects.
  4. Prototype Requirements:
    • Hardware projects: Teams must bring a functional prototype.
    • Software projects: Teams must bring laptops with their software installed and ready to demo.
  5. Project Presentation:
    • Teams will have a specific time limit (communicated prior to the event) to present their projects to the judges.
    • Presentations must include a demo of the project and an explanation of the innovation, creativity, and completion of tasks and challenges.
  6. Any unsportsmanlike conduct, including but not limited to cheating or plagiarism, will result in immediate disqualification.
  7. Teams will be judged on the basis of innovation, creativity, and completion of tasks and challenges.
